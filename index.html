FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about window cleaning, window tinting, and our services in Delray Beach, FL.

How often should I have my windows professionally cleaned?

Most homes in Delray Beach benefit from professional window cleaning every 3 to 6 months due to salt air, humidity, and pollen buildup. Commercial storefronts often look best with monthly or bi-weekly service to maintain a consistently clean appearance for customers.

Do you clean both the inside and outside of windows?

Yes, we offer both interior and exterior window cleaning. Many customers choose exterior-only service for routine upkeep and add interior cleaning periodically, before guests visit, or for a deeper seasonal clean.

Is window cleaning safe for tinted or treated glass?

Yes, we use gentle, streak-free cleaning solutions and techniques that are safe for tinted, treated, and energy-efficient glass commonly found in Florida homes and businesses. We always assess the glass type before starting any job.

Do you offer window tinting in Delray?

Yes, in addition to our window cleaning services, we offer window tinting in Delray to help reduce glare, block heat, and protect your interiors from UV damage year-round. Contact us for details and pricing.

Do you offer commercial window cleaning service?

Yes, we provide commercial window cleaning for offices, retail storefronts, restaurants, and other commercial properties throughout Delray Beach and Palm Beach County, with flexible recurring schedules available.
